# Abhista - Construction & Home Service Management Platform

## 1. Project Vision

To create a unified digital platform that connects customers, contractors, architects, designers, and skilled workers while simplifying construction and home service management through transparency, communication, and project tracking.

Abhista aims to act as a service coordination platform rather than a material marketplace, helping customers manage projects without dealing with construction complexity.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Construction and home service projects often suffer from:

* Lack of transparency in project progress and expenses
* Difficulty finding trusted contractors and professionals
* Poor communication between customers and service providers
* Limited visibility into timelines, milestones, and project completion
* Dependence on manual coordination across multiple stakeholders

Most customers want completed work, not the burden of managing materials, vendors, and technical construction decisions.

---

# 3. Target Users

### Customers

Homeowners and property owners seeking construction, renovation, interior design, maintenance, or repair services.

### Contractors

Construction contractors managing projects, teams, and customer relationships.

### Architects & Designers

Professionals providing design consultation, planning, and interior solutions.

### Skilled Workers

Electricians, plumbers, painters, carpenters, CCTV technicians, and other service professionals.

### Platform Administrators

Internal team responsible for verification, moderation, support, and platform management.

---

# 4. Project Goals

### Primary Goals
